About

Mottr.am is Jack Mottram's weblog.

It's a tumblelog- or microblog-type affair, mostly featuring links to interesting places around the web, images, videos, quotes, with occasional original content.

Colophon

The site is marked up in probably valid HTML5, styled with currently invalid CSS 3, and powered by the excellent static site generator jekyll.

In many browsers, headers are set in Museo Sans 500 using @font-face, thanks to Font Squirrel's @font-face Kit Generator.
